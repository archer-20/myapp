FAQs about Bike Insurance

Is third-party bike insurance mandatory?

Yes, having at least a 3rd party bike insurance policy is mandatory under the Motor Vehicles Act 1988.

What is covered in third-party insurance for bike?

Third party bike insurance covers damage to the third-party property and injury, or death of a third party caused due to your two-wheeler in an accident.

Can I transfer insurance when selling my car?

Yes, you must transfer the insurance to the new owner. Inform the insurer and submit required documents like sale agreement and RC transfer.
